Our Story

A Legacy of
Pure Passion

Founded in 1987 by Vikram Mehra, APEX MOTORS began with a singular conviction: that the finest automobiles deserve the finest stewardship. What started as a single showroom in Mumbai has grown into India's most prestigious automotive destination.

Over three decades, we have placed over 2,400 extraordinary vehicles with clients who understand that driving is not merely transport — it is expression. Every transaction we facilitate is rooted in deep trust, meticulous preparation, and an uncompromising commitment to authenticity.

Today, under the leadership of Aryan Mehra, APEX continues to evolve — embracing the electrified future while honoring the combustion legends that defined us. Our promise remains unchanged: excellence, always.
